Description

maxon GPX gearheads combine extremely high power transmission with a very short construction. The modular design provides the basis for a customer-specific solution. The speed-optimized input stage and the reinforced output enable high power transmission even at high speeds. With the integrated shaft seal, this gearhead is protected against ingress of media and can be sterilized repeatedly.

The mechanical interfaces can be configured.
