Description

maxon GPX gearheads combine extremely high power transmission with a very short construction. The modular design provides the basis for a customer-specific solution. The speed-optimized input stage and the reinforced output enable high power transmission even at high speeds. The gearhead can be sterilized repeatedly.

The mechanical interfaces can be configured.
