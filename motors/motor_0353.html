Description

maxon DCX motors feature unrivaled power density (torque per volume) and absolutely quiet running. The robust engineering, combined with the globally patented ironless winding, make the DCX motors a highly dynamic drive for almost every application scenario.

The DCX 26 L is a powerhouse. Preloaded ball bearings or sintered bearings, graphite or precious metal brushes with CLL – the extensive options make it suitable for a wide range of applications.
