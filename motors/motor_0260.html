Description

maxon DCX motors feature unrivaled power density (torque per volume) with quiet operation. The robust engineering, combined with the globally patented ironless winding, makes the DCX range a highly dynamic and responsive motor for a wide range of applications.

The DCX 16 S offers preloaded ball bearings or sintered bearings, graphite or precious metal brushes, spark suppression (CLL) is also optional. This wide range of configurations form the basis for many applications.
