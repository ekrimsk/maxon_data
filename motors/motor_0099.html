Description

With their optimized magnetic circuit, these brushless EC-i motors with iron windings offer a very high torque density and very low cogging torque. The multipole internal rotor has excellent dynamics. The robust design with a steel flange and housing makes this unit suitable for a wide variety of applications. Order your EC-i drive online now.
